## Academic writing

Academic writing is a difficult skill to acquire, as attention during graduate degrees focuses on the science rather than science communication. This leaves many students unprepared to write their first manuscript. In order to help streamline a first publication I provide some academic writing materials.

I provide an R markdown template on [this page](https://github.com/khufkens/paper_writing_template). This template makes it easier to structure a paper in R markdown and render it as a nicely formatted pdf (to be submitted to a journal).

This R markdown templates follows the rules as outline in my [paper writing checklist](https://github.com/khufkens/paper_writing_checklist), which can be used as a writing style guide or during peer-review.
